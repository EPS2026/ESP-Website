About Us

At Empowered Pathway Services, our mission is to provide compassionate, personalized support to individuals with developmental disabilities, empowering them to achieve independence, build confidence, and live fulfilling lives. We are committed to fostering a safe, inclusive, and encouraging environment where every person can discover their pathway to growth, self-suﬃciency, and meaningful community engagement.

Our Services

Independent Living Skills

Support with cooking, budgeting, hygiene, and daily routines.

Community Integration

Assistance with employment goals, education, and community engagement.

Behavioral Support

Structured guidance and consistent person-centered support.

24/7 On-Call Support

Reliable response and assistance when it matters most.
